BMW (Bayerische Motoren Werke AG) is a German luxury vehicle, motorcycle, and engine manufacturer headquartered in Munich, Germany, founded in 1916, and is known for its high-performance, premium quality, and luxury cars and motorcycles, operating globally through its brands BMW, MINI, and Rolls-Royce. The company has a rich history, initially focusing on aircraft engines before transitioning to automobiles and motorcycles, and now also provides financial and mobility services
